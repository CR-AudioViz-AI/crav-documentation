## Licensing & Rights

### License Types

**Personal License** (Default):
- One user
- Personal projects only
- No commercial use
- No resale
- Price: Standard

**Commercial License:**
- One user
- Commercial projects allowed
- Can use for clients
- No resale
- Price: 2-3x personal

**Extended License:**
- Unlimited users (one company)
- Commercial use
- Resale in end products
- No standalone resale
- Price: 5-10x personal

### What You Must Own

**You Must Have Rights To:**
- All designs and artwork
- All photos and images
- All fonts used
- All audio/video
- All text content

**You Cannot Sell:**
- Work you don't own
- Copyrighted material
- Trademarked content
- Stock assets (without rights)
- AI-generated art (without disclosure)

### License Terms

**When Someone Buys:**
- They get license to use
- You retain copyright
- You can sell to unlimited buyers
- Buyer cannot resell your work
- Buyer cannot claim as their own
