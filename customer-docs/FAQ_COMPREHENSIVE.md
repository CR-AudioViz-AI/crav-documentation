### Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

**A:** Absolutely! Change your plan anytime with these rules:

**Upgrading:** Takes effect immediately. You're charged a prorated amount for the remaining days in your billing cycle, and your new credit allocation starts right away.

**Downgrading:** Takes effect at your next billing cycle. You keep your current plan benefits until then, and any excess credits remain in your account (they don't expire on paid plans).

### Q: Can I cancel anytime?

**A:** Yes! Cancel anytime with no penalties or fees. Here's what happens:
- Your subscription continues until the end of your billing period
- You keep access to all features until then
- Your credits remain in your account (they don't expire on paid plans)
- No prorated refunds (you've been using the service)
- You can reactivate anytime

### Q: What's your refund policy?

**A:** We offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** for new subscribers:
- Request a full refund within 30 days of your first purchase
- No questions asked
- You keep any credits you've already used

**After the first 30 days:**
- No prorated refunds (credits are a consumable product)
- Exception: Platform failure (downtime >24 hours)
- Exception: Billing errors (duplicate charges)

**Note:** If you have a legitimate issue, contact support@craudiovizai.com. We resolve all good-faith disputes promptly and fairly.

### Q: What if my subscription lapses?

**A:** You have a **10-day grace period** to renew and keep all your credits. After that, you have **180 days** to reactivate and restore your credit balance. Beyond 180 days, credits are forfeited.

### Q: Do you auto-renew subscriptions?

**A:** Yes, subscriptions auto-renew to ensure uninterrupted service. You'll receive an email reminder 3 days before your renewal date. You can cancel anytime before the renewal date to avoid charges.
