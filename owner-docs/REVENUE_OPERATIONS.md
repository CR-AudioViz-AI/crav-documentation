## Sales Process

### Self-Service (Free/Starter/Pro)

**Funnel:**
1. Website visit (10,000/month target)
2. Sign up (20% = 2,000)
3. Activate (try tool) (50% = 1,000)
4. Convert to paid (10% = 100)

**Conversion Tactics:**
- Free trial with 50 credits
- In-app upgrade prompts
- Email nurture sequence
- Success stories
- Limited-time offers

### Sales-Assisted (Business/White-Label/Enterprise)

**Process:**
1. Lead qualification
2. Discovery call (30 min)
3. Demo (60 min)
4. Proposal & pricing
5. Negotiation
6. Close

**Team:**
- Month 1-6: Founders
- Month 7-9: 2 sales reps
- Month 10-14: 5 sales reps

### Partnership Sales

**Process:**
1. Identify strategic fit
2. Outreach (warm intro preferred)
3. Exploratory meeting
4. Pilot program (3 months)
5. Partnership agreement
6. Launch & optimize

## Pricing Strategy

### Value-Based Pricing

**Core Principle:**
- Price based on value delivered, not cost
- Professional tools worth $3,000+/year
- We offer at $49-149/month = huge savings
- Credits model = predictable costs for users

### Competitive Analysis

**Competitors:**
- Adobe Creative Cloud: $60/month (single category)
- Canva Pro: $15/month (limited tools)
- Individual tools: $20-100/month each
- **Our advantage: All-in-one at competitive price**

### Discounting Strategy

**Annual Plans:**
- Save 2 months (17% discount)
- Improves cash flow
- Reduces churn

**Volume Discounts:**
- 10-49 users: 10% off
- 50-99 users: 20% off
- 100+ users: Custom pricing

**Promotions:**
- Launch: 50% off first 3 months
- Black Friday: 40% off annual
- Referrals: 1 month free
